What We Do

Tea Society brings students together through fun and relaxing club activities.

Tea Tastings

Explore green tea, black tea, herbal tea, and international blends with other members.

Social Events

Meet new people, share stories, and enjoy a welcoming environment on campus.

Tea Education

Learn about tea traditions, preparation methods, and the cultural history behind them.

Why Join Tea Society?

Tea Society is a place for students who want to relax, meet others, and explore different tea cultures. Whether you are already a tea enthusiast or just curious, everyone is welcome.

Members can participate in club events, make new friends, and become part of a positive campus community.

Club Highlights

  • Weekly tea meetups
  • Seasonal tasting events
  • Student networking and socializing
  • Open to all experience levels
